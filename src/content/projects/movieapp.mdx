## What It Does

- Shows popular movies on first load — no searching needed
- Search by movie title with live results
- Save favorites with a heart toggle (stays checked between sessions)
- Full detail page: runtime, genres, rating, budget, revenue

## Implementation Notes

I used **React Router** for the pages (Home, Favorites, and `/movie/:id` for details). Favorites live in a **MovieContext** so the state is shared predictably across components, and they're synced to **localStorage** — refresh the page and your saved movies are still there.

The hard part was getting the card interactions right. Clicking a card should navigate to the details page, but clicking the heart should only toggle the favorite — not navigate. Took me a couple tries to get that right without confusing the user.

Reusing the same card component for both Home and Favorites turned out to be a good call. Kept the UI consistent and saved me from maintaining two variants.

## What I'd Add Next

- Pagination or infinite scroll (the API has more results than I'm showing)
- Genre/year/rating filters
- Better empty and error states — nothing worse than a broken image placeholder
- User auth so favorites sync across devices instead of living in localStorage
